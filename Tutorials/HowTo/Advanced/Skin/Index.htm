Skins

Skins are used by the menu maker to skin the menu parts. Skins in EGM are very simple to use and make. The system itself is very flexible, especially the dynamic bar.

Skin types are Window, List, Button, Textbox, Cursor, and Dynamic Bar. You can create and use multiple skins by one or more menus, so you are not limited to just one skin. Don’t be afraid to create a new skin for just one dynamic bar or window if you have to.

Open the Skin editor. Add a new skin and choose Window from the drop down list. To continue, you’ll need to import the window skins from any of the templates.

Pick the appropriate skins and you’ll see the Window in the preview area change.

Making skins is easy. All you have to do is divide your skin into parts determined by the skin editor. For example, for the Window, we have Background, Top Left, Top Right, Top Center, Left, Right, Bottom Left, Bottom Center, and Bottom Right. These parts are all small but the engine repeats them to make them whole.
