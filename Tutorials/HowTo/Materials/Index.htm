Importing Materials

Materials are images, audios, videos and fonts that you can use in your game.

In order to import your own material, first move your mouse over to the Materials tab. If not visible, go to View and click on Material Explorer. You should see:

The white icon allows you to import resources. Click on it and find an image you would like to import. Double click it to add it in.

You can also add your own folders and organize your files and folders by dragging and dropping. You can also cut/paste instead of drag and drop.
