/* -----------------------------
 * Dockables
 * -----------------------------
 * Contains all the dockable editors and the dialogs they use.
 * Classes - These classes are used by all the editors to load content (images, audio), Interfaces, and to hold more data.
 * Components - These components are used by the editors and dialogs.
