Collisions (Events)

Collision for events can be defined in the Animation editor.

  1. Select the animation and action you wish to add a collision box.
  2. Click and new options will display.

  3. There are different tools for you to easily create a collision box. For this tutorial click on "Add Circle"
  4. Next click left of the sprite and drag it from left to right. Once you let go, a yellow circle should appear.
For most cases, a circle is best, especially for moving characters. However, for static objects, rectangles and "Sprite Outline" is best. To get the hang of it, you should experiment what kind of collision shape is best for you.

The is for battle collision, mainly for projectiles. It can be used the same way and the recommended option is "Rectangle" or "Sprite Outline".

If your collision has too many nodes, simplify it by clicking .

If it doesn't have enough, collisions won't be accurate. Click to subdivide.

To add a new node to an existing shape, hold [SHIFT], click on a node and move your mouse.
