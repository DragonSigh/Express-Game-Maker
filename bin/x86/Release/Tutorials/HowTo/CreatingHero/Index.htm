Creating a Hero

Heroes are essential to your game. A hero is a representation of the player and so in order to have a player, you'll need at least one hero.

  1. Open the Heroes editor by clicking .
  2. Add a new hero by clicking green PLUS sign.
  3. Add the heroes Animation by double clicking the gray box.
  4. Assign Idle and Walk actions by clicking "Assign Actions" button.
    • You only need Idle, which can be a still of the heroe's sprite or an actual idle animation, and you need a Walk animation. The idle animation can simply be made from Walk animation by removing the "step" frames.
  5. Once the actions are assigned, you can use the hero as a player

There is a lot more you can do to heroes. To learn more, check out tutorials for specific genres such as the RPG tutorials.
